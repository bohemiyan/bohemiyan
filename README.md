## Who I Am

I founded **[Kriviya Technologies](https://kriviya.com)** to solve a real problem — India's fragmented agricultural trade. Today, Kriviya is a commodity trading platform that connects farmers directly with traders and buyers, cutting out middlemen and ensuring transparent, fair pricing.

Alongside that, I co-built **[Veryfi.in](https://veryfi.in)** — a plug-and-play KYC verification API suite that gives businesses access to identity verification infrastructure at the guaranteed lowest market price.

I don't just lead — I architect, build, and ship.

## Ventures

### 🌾 [Kriviya Technologies Pvt Ltd](https://kriviya.com)
**Founder & CEO**

India's transparent agricultural commodity trading platform — bridging the gap between farmers, traders, and buyers with technology.

- Real-time commodity price discovery and trade execution
- Direct farmer-to-buyer pipeline, eliminating exploitative middlemen
- IoT-enabled supply chain tracking and analytics
- Enterprise-grade infrastructure built for Bharat's scale

### 🪪 [Veryfi.in](https://veryfi.in)
**Co-Founder**

A developer-first KYC verification API & suite platform — built for businesses that need reliable identity verification without the enterprise price tag.

- Plug-and-play REST APIs for Aadhaar, PAN, Bank, GST & more
- Guaranteed lowest market pricing — no hidden costs
- Built for fast integration — go live in hours, not weeks
- Scalable infrastructure trusted for compliance-grade verification
